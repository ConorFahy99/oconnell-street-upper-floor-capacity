# CSO census data

Source: Central Statistics Office
Datasets: SAPS 2022 (Small Area Population Statistics) + Small Area boundaries

## Purpose
Confirm near-zero residential population in the study area.
Used as a framing stat in the pitch: buildings tall enough for many residents, currently housing almost none.

## Download
- Small Area boundaries: CSO boundary files (GeoPackage or Shapefile)
- SAPS 2022: population table, joined to Small Area boundaries in QGIS

## Files expected here
- `small_areas_2022.gpkg` (or .shp)
- `SAPS2022_*.csv`
